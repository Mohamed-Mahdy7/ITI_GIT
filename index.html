Park Guell

The benches as Lizard fountain in Park Guell make up part of the UNESCO World Heritage Site known as, "The Works of Antonio Gaudí."

The park features Gaudí's famous Lizard Fountain, as well as benches and other extraordinary examples of Gaudí's talent with tiles.
